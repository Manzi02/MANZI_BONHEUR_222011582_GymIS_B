Gym Information System

  1. DATABASE

    2. Database name used: Gym Information System

    • Tables

      1. User table

        2. User table stores information about the users of the system, such as their username, password, email address, role. So that Existing users can log in by providing their username and password.

      2. Trainer

        3. The trainer table stores information about trainers who work within the gym: the trainer's ID, name, contact information and their specialization. Also this table will contain their availability and accept information from new trainer register.

      3. MEMBER

        4. This table stores information about individuals who are members of the gym, it contains details such as the member's ID, name, contact information, address, date of birth, and other personal information. This table will also accept information of new member (registration) and show the payment information(methods)

      4. PAYMENT

        5. :The PAYMENT table manages financial transactions and records related to members' payments within the gym information system, such as unique payment ID, the member's ID, payment date, payment amount, and payment methods.

      5. EQUIPMENT

        6. This table contains details about the drivers who fulfill fuel delivery orders through the app. It may include driver names, contact information, vehicle details, delivery schedules, performance metrics, etc. The table facilitates driver management, assignment of delivery tasks, tracking driver activities, and optimizing delivery routes.


      6. CLASS SCHEDULET

        7. This table in the gym information system manages the schedule of classes offered by the gym, each class offered by the gym is assigned a unique Class ID within the table, facilitating easy identification. This table also show date, start time, duration and It allows staff to plan and organize classes effectively


      7. ATTENDANCE

        8. Attendance table in the gym information system is responsible for tracking the attendance of members in various classes offered by the gym also includes details such as the member's ID, the class ID, the date of attendance. The table records the date on which the attendance was taken like Check in time and check out time.
